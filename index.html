What is the problem?

Non-verbal autistics are unable to communicate effectively with their caretakers and others around them. This can prevent them from conveying important information such as in an the case of an emergency, or when they have other needs. Additionally, other autism apps with similar purposes rely on developers to expand upon the dictionary of available words, which is limiting for the user. Finally many communication apps are only available in one environment, whereas ours is available on all platforms.

What we provide:

The Inner Voice app is an expandable voice framework app that helps children who are non-verbal autistics communicate. The app supports new words being added in by both the user and their caretaker. Users create each sentence by selecting the main idea or subject first, and then expanding upon that to form a complete sentence. The structure of the sentences used by a user, with the explicit consent of their caretaker, can be recorded and sent to a family doctor or a researcher studying autism to further their studies.
